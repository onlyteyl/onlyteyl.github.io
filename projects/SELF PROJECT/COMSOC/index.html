Our Story

Legacy of the Computer Society

For over three decades, the Computer Society has served as the academic and technical home of IT students at PUP Sto. Tomas Campus. What began as a small circle of computing enthusiasts has grown into a full-fledged organization anchoring seminars, competitions, and outreach programs across the university.

Guided by generations of student leaders and faculty advisers, COMSOC continues to champion academic excellence, industry readiness, and a culture of service — values reflected in every initiative it undertakes, from technical bootcamps to community-driven digital literacy drives.
