DETAILS
A14 BIONIC CHIP
The A14 Bionic chip is the iPhone 12 Pro's powerhouse, delivering lightning-fast performance and efficiency. Built with a 5-nanometer process, it features a 6-core CPU and a 4-core GPU, making multitasking, gaming, and editing smooth and responsive. With its advanced Neural Engine, the A14 Bionic enables faster machine learning, powering features like enhanced photography and augmented reality. It's the smartest and most efficient chip ever in an iPhone, offering top-tier performance and energy efficiency.
CAMERA
The iPhone 12 Pro's camera system features a pro-grade triple-lens setup with 12MP Ultra Wide, Wide, and Telephoto lenses, capturing stunning detail in any light. The Night mode ensures bright, clear shots in low-light conditions, while Deep Fusion enhances textures and details. With 4K Dolby Vision HDR recording, your videos come to life with incredible color and clarity. The LiDAR scanner also enables faster autofocus and enhances AR experiences, making the iPhone 12 Pro's camera one of the most advanced yet.
XDR-DISPLAY
The iPhone 12 Pro features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, delivering breathtaking clarity and vibrant colors. With its OLED technology, the screen offers deep blacks, bright whites, and a 2,000,000:1 contrast ratio. It supports HDR10 and Dolby Vision for an immersive viewing experience, whether you're watching videos or browsing photos. The Ceramic Shield provides four times better drop performance, making it both stunning and durable.
